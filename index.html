Welcome to my Portfolio

I am a considerate and creative collaborator based out of Oakland, CA.

I love stories.

I love consuming them, making them, recycling them, picking them apart and putting them back together. Stories are the means by which we structure the world around us. They are how we connect the dots between Point A and Point B. Every solution to every problem is a story, whether it be a dialogue or an algorithm, a game or a song.

It has been my pleasure to participate in many storytelling projects over the years. This page collects a few of my favorites.
